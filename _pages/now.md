**Updated March 2026**

Writing this from a Juiceland, enjoying a Soul Boulder, on my way to dance country two-step this evening. I have become somewhat two-step obsessed after finally learning more than just a handful of basic patterns. It gets more fun the better you get. 

I'm in my third month at Superbuilders, designing and piloting a course at [Alpha High School.](https://alphahigh.school/). The main educational objective is social media usage—how to build a following, self-express authentically, and connect with others. All my social media posting, life coaching, and connecting is coming together! 

During Gauntlet last fall, I started holding a plank each morning. The morning plank is now five minutes. This past weekened, I went for gold and got to twelve minutes. My new favorite morning practice: plank hold, then journal + meditate. After we move to our new office, I intend to start training the planche again in earnest. The gymnast is making a come back.

Declaring that by my next /now/ update, I will have started a public self-expression project again. Last year's daily instagram posting was *such* a good experiment-learned so much about myself, connected with so many people, and got great feedback. Round 2, let's go.
